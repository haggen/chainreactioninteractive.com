The steps towards a successful game project

  1. Initial quote

    Once we have a briefing we'll analyze what your project needs and provide you with a quote, all free of charge.

  2. Pre-production

    In this step we'll begin assembling the team that'll work on your project and studying its details. We'll provide you with a detailed estimate and roadmap of its production. If you're starting from scratch we'll also create a proper “Game Overview Document”, to make sure our vision is crystal clear. A good pre-production can save enormous amounts of time on the long run since it's when we point out and prepare for potential issues and prepare a good strategy to complete your project on time and within budget.

  3. Production

    This step is when we go full-on on programming, developing the gameplay, producing art assets any other features your game might need. All the while our team manager will be keeping you posted with weekly checkpoints and scheduled meetings to make sure we are on track with the expectations aligned. We use Scrum and Agile workflows to maintain communication, flexibility and transparency throughout the project.

  4. Release and post-release support

    Once your game is ready we'll help you with the publishing process to stores such as Google Play, App Store, Steam and Epic. But our work doesn't stop there, as, in any software project, post-release support is just as important, and we'll continue working on patches and improvements, even after the game is out for the public.
